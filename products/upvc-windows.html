Frequently Asked Questions

What glass do you use in UPVC windows?

We exclusively use Saint-Gobain glass in all our UPVC windows. Options include: Single glass (5-12mm) for standard applications, Double Glazed Unit DGU (16-22mm) for enhanced thermal and sound insulation, and Laminated glass for enhanced safety and superior soundproofing. Saint-Gobain is a world-renowned glass manufacturer known for superior quality.

What hardware do you use in UPVC windows?

We use only imported hardware in all our UPVC windows for superior quality, durability, and smooth operation. This includes multi-point locks, handles, hinges, rollers, and all moving parts. Imported hardware ensures long-lasting performance, minimal maintenance, and superior security compared to local alternatives.

How much sound insulation do UPVC windows provide?

Our UPVC windows with Saint-Gobain DGU glass provide sound insulation up to 45dB, making them ideal for homes near busy roads, airports, or noisy areas. This significantly reduces external noise for peaceful living. Single glass provides 25-30dB reduction, while laminated glass offers even better soundproofing.

Do UPVC windows require maintenance?

UPVC windows require minimal maintenance. Simply wipe with soap and water occasionally, lubricate imported hardware once a year, and clean drainage holes annually. No painting, no rusting, no termite damage. With imported hardware and Saint-Gobain glass, UPVC windows maintain their appearance and functionality for decades with minimal care.
