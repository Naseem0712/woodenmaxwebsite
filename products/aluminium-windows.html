  • Profile Thickness: 1.2mm to 2.0mm (varies by design)

Saint-Gobain Glass Options

  • Single Glass: 5mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm thickness

Frequently Asked Questions

Which aluminium profiles do you use for windows?

We use premium Hindalco aluminium profiles and imported profiles for our aluminium windows. Both are high-quality options - Hindalco for standard applications and imported profiles for specialized custom designs and premium projects. The choice depends on your specific requirements and budget.

What type of glass do you use in aluminium windows?

We exclusively use Saint-Gobain glass in all our aluminium windows. Options include: Single glass (5-12mm) for standard applications, Double Glazed Unit DGU (16-22mm) for enhanced thermal and sound insulation, and Laminated glass for enhanced safety and superior soundproofing.

Do you provide powder coating on aluminium windows?

Yes, we provide premium powder coating on aluminium windows, but only on imported profiles. Powder coating is available in any RAL color to match your design requirements. Standard Hindalco profiles come in standard finishes (anodized silver, standard colors). For custom colors, imported profiles with powder coating are recommended.

What hardware do you use in aluminium windows?

We use only imported hardware in all our aluminium windows for superior quality, durability, and smooth operation. This includes hinges, handles, locks, rollers, and all moving parts. Imported hardware ensures long-lasting performance and minimal maintenance requirements.

Can you customize aluminium window designs?

Yes, we specialize in customized aluminium window designs. We can create unique profiles, custom sizes, special shapes, and match any architectural requirement. Our imported profiles allow for maximum design flexibility. Whether you need curved windows, special angles, or unique configurations, we can manufacture them to your exact specifications.

What is the difference between Hindalco and imported profiles?

Hindalco profiles are premium Indian-made aluminium profiles, excellent quality and cost-effective for standard applications. Imported profiles offer superior design flexibility, custom powder coating options, and are ideal for specialized projects requiring unique shapes or colors. Both are high-quality; the choice depends on your specific needs.

Yes, all our aluminium windows come with a comprehensive 10-year warranty covering profiles, glass, and hardware. The warranty covers manufacturing defects, material quality, and performance issues.
