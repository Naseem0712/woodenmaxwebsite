Frequently Asked Questions

What types of aluminium windows do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range: 3-track sliding windows (with/without mesh), 2-track French sliding doors (with top fixed glass arch), top-hung casement windows (outward opening with multipoint lock), Georgian grill casement doors (with golden patterns), French doors with Georgian bar (35mm/40mm slim profiles), slim entrance glass doors (40mm super luxury), aluminium sliding windows, and full elevation villa facades.

Which aluminium profiles do you use?

We use premium Hindalco aluminium profiles (6063 T6 grade) for standard applications and imported profiles for custom designs and specialized projects. Both offer superior strength and durability. Hindalco is cost-effective while imported profiles offer design flexibility and custom powder coating options.

What type of glass do you use?

We exclusively use Saint-Gobain glass: Single glass (5-12mm) for standard applications, Double Glazed Unit DGU (16-22mm) for enhanced thermal and sound insulation, Laminated glass for safety and soundproofing, and Safety glass (13.52mm) for high-security applications.

Do you provide powder coating and color options?

Yes, premium powder coating is available on imported profiles in any RAL color. Ready stock colors include Dark Grey, Matt Black, and Champion Gold. Other RAL colors are available on order. Standard Hindalco profiles come with anodized silver and standard finishes.

What hardware do you use?

We use 100% imported hardware in all our aluminium windows - including hinges, handles, locks, rollers, and friction stays. Imported hardware ensures smooth operation, durability, and superior security. Premium multipoint hardware upgrades automatically for heavy glass options.
