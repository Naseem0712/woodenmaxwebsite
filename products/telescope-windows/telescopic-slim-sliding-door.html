Product Description

The Telescopic Slim Profile Sliding Door is the perfect solution for kitchen partitions, living room partitions, hall partitions, and dining area separations. This innovative multi-panel sliding door system uses imported soft-close hardware that ensures silent, smooth operation every time.

Telescopic Panel Configurations

Choose from multiple telescopic configurations: 1+1 (1 sliding + 1 fixed), 2+1 (2 sliding + 1 fixed), 3+1 (3 sliding + 1 fixed), and 4+1 (4 sliding + 1 fixed). The telescopic mechanism allows multiple panels to slide together in one direction, creating a wide opening while taking minimal wall space.

Imported Slim Profiles

We offer three imported slim profile options: Ultra Slim 12X35mm for maximum glass visibility, Slim 16X35mm for balanced aesthetics, and Regular Slim 16X45mm for enhanced durability. All profiles are made from premium 6063-T6 grade aluminium.

Premium Glass Options

Base rate includes 8mm clear tuff glass. Premium options include 8mm clear fluted glass, 8mm grey fluted glass, and 8mm brown fluted glass - all imported for superior quality. Fluted glass provides privacy while allowing light to pass through, perfect for kitchen partitions.

Imported Soft-Close Hardware

All hardware is imported with soft-close technology for silent operation. Includes D-type handle, premium rollers, and track system. 2 years warranty on hardware with 1 year free service.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a telescopic sliding door?
A telescopic sliding door is a multi-panel door system where multiple shutters slide together in one direction. When you open one panel, the others follow automatically, creating a wide opening while taking minimal wall space. It's perfect for kitchen partitions, living room partitions, and hall partitions.
What panel configurations are available?
We offer four telescopic configurations: 1+1 (1 sliding + 1 fixed), 2+1 (2 sliding + 1 fixed), 3+1 (3 sliding + 1 fixed), and 4+1 (4 sliding + 1 fixed). The choice depends on your opening width and design preference. Each configuration includes imported soft-close hardware.
What profile options are available?
We offer three imported slim profile options: Ultra Slim 12X35mm for maximum glass visibility, Slim 16X35mm for balanced aesthetics, and Regular Slim 16X45mm for enhanced durability. All profiles are made from premium 6063-T6 grade imported aluminium.
Can this door reduce cooking smell from kitchen?
Yes! With tight gasket fitting and glass partition, the cooking smell reduces significantly. The sealed design prevents odors, dust, and noise from spreading to your living room and other areas. This makes it perfect for kitchen partitions.
Which glass is best for kitchen partition?
Fluted Glass is the most popular choice! It provides privacy while allowing light to pass through. We offer 8mm clear fluted, grey fluted, and brown fluted glass - all imported for superior quality. The base rate includes 8mm clear tuff glass.
Is soft-close mechanism available?
Yes, all panels come with imported soft-close sliding hardware as standard. This ensures silent, smooth operation and prevents slamming. The hardware includes D-type handle and premium rollers. We provide 2 years warranty on hardware with 1 year free service.
What color options are available?
We offer four premium color options: Matt Black, Gold, Rose Gold, and Copper Gold. All finishes are weather-resistant and maintain their appearance for years without fading.
What is the warranty period?
We provide 2 years warranty on all imported hardware (soft-close mechanism, rollers, handles) and 1 year free service. The aluminium profiles are built to last for decades without rusting or fading.
