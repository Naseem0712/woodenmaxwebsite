‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is a telescopic sliding door?
A telescopic sliding door is a multi-panel door system where 2 or 3 shutters slide together in one direction to save space. When you open one panel, the others follow automatically, creating a wide opening while taking minimal wall space.
Can this door reduce cooking smell from kitchen?
Yes! With tight gasket fitting + glass partition, the smell from cooking reduces significantly. The sealed design prevents odors from spreading to your living room and other areas.
Which glass is best for kitchen partition?
Fluted Glass is the most popular choice! It gives privacy + stylish look + hides kitchen clutter while still allowing light to pass through. It's the perfect balance of aesthetics and functionality.
Is soft-close mechanism available?
Yes, all panels come with imported soft-close sliding hardware as standard. This ensures silent, smooth operation and prevents slamming, making it safe for homes with children.
What panel configurations are available?
We offer three telescopic configurations: 1+1 (2 panels), 2+1 (3 panels), and 3+1 (4 panels). The choice depends on your opening width and design preference.
What is the warranty period?
We provide 10 years warranty on aluminium profiles and 5 years warranty on hardware and soft-close mechanism. Glass is covered under manufacturing defects.
