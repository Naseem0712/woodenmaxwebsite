Product Description

The Fold & Sliding Window System is a premium solution featuring imported slim aluminium profiles and imported hardware. This system is designed for 2-door configurations and is perfect for creating elegant gates, entrance doors, and window openings with a modern, slim aesthetic.

Slim Profile Design

The imported slim profiles give this system an ultra-modern, minimalist look. The slim frame maximizes glass visibility while maintaining structural strength. This creates a seamless, elegant appearance that enhances any architectural style.

Ideal for Gates & Entrance Doors

This system is generally used for gates and entrance doors where you need both style and security. The 2-door sliding configuration allows for wide openings while maintaining a compact footprint. Perfect for main entrance gates, garden gates, balcony doors, and premium window openings.

Premium Glass Options

Choose from multiple glass options: 8mm clear tuff glass (included in base rate), 10mm clear tuff glass for enhanced strength, and imported fluted glass (8mm clear, grey, brown) for privacy with style.

Security with Mortice Lock

Every unit comes with a mortice lock included in the base rate, providing excellent security for your gates and doors. The imported hardware ensures smooth operation and long-lasting durability.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Fold & Sliding Window System?
The Fold & Sliding Window System is a premium 2-door sliding system featuring imported slim aluminium profiles and imported hardware. It's designed for creating elegant gates, entrance doors, and window openings with a modern, minimalist look while maintaining security with a mortice lock.
Can this system be used for gates?
Yes! This system is generally used for gates and entrance doors. The 2-door sliding configuration with mortice lock makes it perfect for main entrance gates, garden gates, villa entrances, and any area where you need both style and security.
What glass options are available?
We offer multiple glass options: 8mm clear tuff glass (included in base rate), 10mm clear tuff glass for enhanced strength, and imported fluted glass (8mm clear, grey, brown) for privacy with elegance. All glass options are toughened for safety.
What color options are available?
We offer four premium color options: Matt Black, Gold, Rose Gold, and Copper Gold. All colors are imported finishes that maintain their appearance for years. Rose Gold and Copper Gold are premium options.
Is mortice lock included?
Yes! The mortice lock is included in the base rate. This provides excellent security for your gates and entrance doors without any additional cost.
Why are imported profiles better?
Imported slim profiles offer several advantages: ultra-slim design for maximum glass visibility, superior finish quality that doesn't fade, precise engineering for smooth operation, and better durability compared to local alternatives. The slim look creates a modern, elegant aesthetic.
Can only 2 doors be made with this system?
Yes, this specific system is designed for 2-door configurations only. This is ideal for gates and entrance doors where you need a wide opening with two sliding panels. For more panels, please check our Bi-Fold Doors which offer 2, 3, 4, and 6 fold options.
What is included in the base rate?
The base rate includes: imported slim aluminium profiles, 8mm clear tuff glass, mortice lock, and imported hardware. Upgrades to 10mm glass or fluted glass are available at additional cost. Contact us for exact pricing.
