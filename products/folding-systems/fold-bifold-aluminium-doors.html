Product Description

The Fold & Bi-Fold Aluminium Glass Door is a versatile, space-saving solution that provides 95% opening space. Unlike traditional doors that swing open and block space, bi-fold doors fold neatly to one side, maximizing your usable area while maintaining a premium, modern look.

Multiple Applications

This bi-fold aluminium door is perfect for multiple applications: balcony doors (connect indoor-outdoor seamlessly), window openings (convert windows to wide openings), living room partitions (divide spaces elegantly), office cabin partitions (create flexible workspaces), showroom fronts (maximize display visibility), restaurant & cafe fronts (open-air dining experience), and garden access doors (bring nature inside).

Premium Aluminium Profiles

Choose between two premium profile options: 50mm profile for standard applications and 52mm profile for enhanced strength. Both profiles are made from high-grade 6063-T6 aluminium with powder coating finish.

Glass Options for Every Need

We offer multiple glass options: 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm clear toughened glass for standard use, Safety Glass (Laminated) for high-security areas and child safety, and DGU Glass for thermal insulation and noise reduction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a bi-fold door?
A bi-fold door (also called folding door) is a door system where multiple panels are connected by hinges and fold together like an accordion. When opened, the panels stack neatly to one side, providing up to 95% opening space. Unlike sliding doors that only provide 50% opening, bi-fold doors maximize your usable space.
What profile options are available?
We offer two aluminium profile options: 50mm profile for standard applications and 52mm profile for enhanced strength. Both profiles are made from premium 6063-T6 grade aluminium which provides excellent strength, durability, and corrosion resistance.
Which glass option should I choose?
Choose based on your needs: 8mm clear tuff glass (included in base rate) for standard use, 10mm or 12mm for larger doors, Safety Glass (Laminated) for high-traffic areas, child safety, or ground floor installations, and DGU Glass for thermal insulation and noise reduction. Safety glass is highly recommended for balcony doors and areas with children.
What color options are available?
We do not keep ready stock colors. All doors are made to order with RAL color powder coating. You can choose any RAL color to match your interior or exterior design. Popular choices include Matt Black, Grey, White, Bronze, Champagne, and Wood Finish textures.
Can bi-fold doors be used on balconies?
Yes! Bi-fold doors are ideal for balconies, terraces, and gardens. They provide maximum opening to connect your indoor and outdoor spaces seamlessly. When fully opened, you get almost complete access to your balcony. We recommend using Safety Glass for balcony installations for added security.
Can I use bi-fold doors for windows?
Absolutely! Bi-fold doors can replace traditional windows to create wide, fully openable spaces. This is perfect for kitchen windows opening to a garden, living room windows with a view, or any area where you want maximum ventilation and light. The folding mechanism allows you to fully open the space when needed and close it securely when not in use.
Are bi-fold doors weather and dust proof?
Yes, our bi-fold doors feature tight gasket sealing and multi-point locking that provides excellent protection against rain, dust, and noise. The aluminium profile system with proper weatherstripping ensures your home stays protected in all weather conditions.
What is included in the base rate?
The base rate includes: 50mm/52mm aluminium profile, 8mm clear tuff glass, mortice lock, and standard hardware. RAL color powder coating is included. Upgrades to thicker glass, safety glass, or DGU glass are available at additional cost. Contact us for exact pricing.
