Product Description

The Full Elevation Aluminium Windows & Doors system offers fixed glass elevation perfect for spaces where you want a luxury full glass view. This system uses imported slim profiles that create a unique, premium feel with maximum glass visibility. Ideal for living room partitions, office partitions, balcony partitions, front elevation, backyard partitions, skylights, and full-height fixed panels in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur.

Perfect for Partitions

This system is ideal for living room partitions to create separate spaces while maintaining visual connectivity, office partitions for modern workspace designs, balcony partitions for outdoor-indoor separation, and interior partitions throughout your home or commercial space.

Imported Slim Profiles

We use premium imported slim profiles (6063-T6 grade) that provide exceptional strength while maintaining an ultra-thin appearance. These profiles are specifically designed for fixed glass applications, creating a seamless, modern look that maximizes natural light and provides unobstructed views.

Premium Glass Options

Choose from a wide range of glass options: Safety glass, DGU glass, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm clear glass, and imported fluted glass options (6mm & 8mm in clear, brown, grey). The base rate includes 6mm clear glass. Fluted glass provides decorative patterns while maintaining transparency - clear fluted (max 10 feet), brown/grey fluted (max 8 feet).

Premium Color Finishes

Ready stock colors include Matt Black, Gold, Brush Gold, Grey, Mill Finish, Rose Gold, and Copper Gold. For custom color requirements, we offer powder coating in any RAL color (available on order). All finishes are weather-resistant and maintain their appearance for years.

Maximum Height 12 Feet

This system is designed with a maximum height of 12 feet for optimal structural stability.

Which glass is best for luxury home elevation?
Laminated DGU Toughened Glass (Safety + Sound + Heat Control). This combination provides excellent safety features, superior sound insulation, and effective heat control, making it ideal for luxury home elevations where performance and aesthetics are equally important.
How strong are slim aluminium profiles?
Structural 6063-T6 grade is used for high-rise & villa engineering. Despite their slim appearance, these profiles are engineered to meet strict structural requirements. The 6063-T6 grade aluminium provides exceptional strength-to-weight ratio, making it suitable for high-rise buildings and luxury villas where both aesthetics and structural integrity are critical.
Does aluminium fade or rust?
No rust ever. Powder coat & anodized finishes last for years without fading. Aluminium is naturally rust-proof, and when combined with high-quality powder coating or anodized finishes, it maintains its appearance for decades. These finishes are specifically designed to resist UV rays, weather conditions, and environmental factors that could cause fading or deterioration.
