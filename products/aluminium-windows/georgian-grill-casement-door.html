Can this aluminium door be used as a main door?
Yes, with laminated/toughened glass + multipoint locking + grill combination. The strong 6063 T6 aluminium profile and multipoint locking system provide the security needed for main entrance doors. Combined with laminated or toughened glass, it offers excellent protection for your home.
Does the Georgian grill block visibility?
No. It adds luxury design without reducing visibility or light. The Georgian grill pattern is decorative and enhances the door's aesthetic appeal while maintaining full visibility and natural light flow through the glass panels.
