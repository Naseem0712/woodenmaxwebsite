Product Description

The Aluminium Sliding Window with Mesh combines sleek modern design with practical functionality. Perfect for homes requiring ventilation without insects, this window features smooth gliding operation and premium materials.

Smooth Gliding Operation

Features imported hardware with precision-engineered rollers for effortless sliding. The window glides smoothly on high-quality tracks, ensuring years of trouble-free operation.

Integrated Insect Mesh

Optional integrated bug mesh keeps insects out while allowing fresh air in. The mesh is seamlessly integrated into the frame design, maintaining the sleek appearance.

Premium 29mm Series Specifications

The 29mm sliding series features robust construction with 1.2mm wall thickness for all sections including track and shutters, ensuring exceptional durability and structural integrity. The shutter sections are precisely engineered with 29X65mm dimensions, providing optimal strength and performance.

This series incorporates a slim interlock mechanism positioned in the middle section, delivering a premium luxury feel and seamless operation. The slim interlock design not only enhances the aesthetic appeal but also ensures smooth sliding action with superior weather sealing, making it perfect for modern architectural applications.
