Product Description

The Slimline Aluminium Window Elite represents the pinnacle of modern window design, combining ultra-slim profiles with premium materials for unmatched performance and aesthetics.

Premium Materials

This window features Hindalco or Imported aluminium profiles - both options offer superior strength and durability. The imported profiles allow for custom powder coating in any RAL color, while Hindalco profiles provide excellent value with standard finishes.

Saint-Gobain Glass

We exclusively use Saint-Gobain glass in all configurations. Choose from single glass (5-12mm) for standard applications, Double Glazed Unit DGU (16-22mm) for enhanced thermal and sound insulation, or laminated glass for superior safety and soundproofing.

Imported Hardware

All hardware components are 100% imported, ensuring smooth operation, superior security with multi-point locking, and long-lasting performance. The imported hardware requires minimal maintenance and provides years of trouble-free operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Hindalco and Imported profiles?

Hindalco profiles are premium Indian-made aluminium profiles, excellent quality and cost-effective. Imported profiles offer superior design flexibility and custom powder coating options in any RAL color. Both are high-quality; choose based on your design requirements and budget.

Which glass type should I choose?

Single glass (5-12mm) is ideal for standard applications. DGU glass (16-22mm) provides better thermal and sound insulation - recommended for bedrooms and areas near noise. Laminated glass offers enhanced safety and superior soundproofing for high-noise areas.

Can I get custom colors?

Yes, custom powder coating is available on imported profiles in any RAL color. This allows you to match your architectural design perfectly. Standard Hindalco profiles come in anodized silver and standard colors.
