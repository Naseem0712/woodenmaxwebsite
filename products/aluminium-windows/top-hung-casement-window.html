Frequently Asked Questions

Can we use mosquito mesh in top hung casement windows?
Yes, inside openable mesh can be added to top hung aluminium casement windows without blocking airflow. The mesh option is perfect for mosquito protection while maintaining maximum ventilation. Available in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur, Lucknow and all major cities. You can choose between inside openable mesh or fixed mesh based on your preference for bathroom windows and kitchen windows.
Does the top hung casement window leak during rain?
No. The top hung design with 40mm casement profile and double gaskets provides complete rain protection. The outward opening design with premium sealing ensures that no water enters your home, even during heavy rainfall. The double gasket system creates a watertight seal, making it perfect for bathroom casement windows and ventilator windows in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur.
Does the multipoint locking system improve safety in top hung windows?
Yes. The multipoint locking system in top hung aluminium casement windows locks the window at multiple points, making it harder to break. The multipoint lock engages multiple locking points along the window frame, providing enhanced security compared to single point lock (handle type). This makes it significantly more difficult for intruders to force open the window. Available with friction stay for smooth operation.
Can we install grills with top hung casement windows?
Absolutely. Top hung casement windows work with both interior and exterior grills. You can choose to install inside aluminium 12mm hollow pipes grills for additional security while maintaining the window's functionality. The grills can be installed on the inside or outside, depending on your security needs and aesthetic preferences. Perfect for bathroom windows and kitchen windows in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur, Lucknow.
What glass options are available for top hung casement windows?
We offer multiple glass options for top hung aluminium casement windows: 6mm clear toughened glass (included), 8mm, 10mm, 12mm clear toughened glass, DGU (Double Glazed Unit), and Laminated glass. Selecting 10mm, 12mm, DGU, or Laminated glass automatically upgrades the hardware to multipoint lock system for enhanced security. Available in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur, Lucknow.

What is the difference between single point lock and multipoint lock in top hung windows?
Single point lock (handle type) is included as standard with top hung casement windows - it locks at one point. Multipoint lock automatically applies when you select 10mm, 12mm, DGU, or Laminated glass - it locks at multiple points along the frame for enhanced security.
