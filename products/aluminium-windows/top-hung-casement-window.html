Product Description

The Top Hung Aluminium Casement Window offers premium ventilation and safety, perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, offices, and ventilator spaces. The window opens smoothly with imported hinges & friction stays, making it ideal for areas where maximum airflow is needed.

Outward Opening Design

The outward opening design allows maximum airflow while keeping the inside space free and stylish. This design is perfect for bathrooms and kitchens where you need ventilation without compromising interior space.

Multipoint Locking System

Secured with a multipoint locking system, this window locks at multiple points, making it harder to break and providing enhanced security for your home or office.

Flexible Options

You can choose with mesh or without mesh, and even add strong safety grills if needed. The sliding or fixed mesh can be added without blocking airflow, making it perfect for mosquito protection while maintaining ventilation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we use a mosquito mesh in top hung windows?
Yes, sliding or fixed mesh can be added without blocking airflow. The mesh option is perfect for mosquito protection while maintaining maximum ventilation. You can choose between sliding mesh or fixed mesh based on your preference.
Does the window leak during rain?
No. Top hung design + double gaskets = complete rain protection. The outward opening design with premium sealing ensures that no water enters your home, even during heavy rainfall. The double gasket system creates a watertight seal.
Does the multipoint lock improve safety?
Yes. It locks the window at multiple points, making it harder to break. The multipoint locking system engages multiple locking points along the window frame, providing enhanced security compared to single-point locks. This makes it significantly more difficult for intruders to force open the window.
Can we install grills with a top hung window?
Absolutely. It works with both interior and exterior grills. You can choose to install grills for additional security while maintaining the window's functionality. The grills can be installed on the inside or outside, depending on your security needs and aesthetic preferences.
