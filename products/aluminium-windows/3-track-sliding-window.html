Product Description

The 3 Track Aluminium Sliding Window is engineered for smooth performance, practical ventilation, and long-lasting durability. Perfect for balconies, bedrooms, living areas, and large openings.

Flexible Mesh Option

This window comes with two sliding glass panels + 1 sliding mesh option, giving you comfortable ventilation without insects, dust, or pollution. You can also choose without-mesh for full-view openings and modern interiors. Choose based on your room requirement - with mesh for balconies & bedrooms, or without mesh for large living windows.
