Product Description

The 3 Track Aluminium Sliding Window (27MM Domal Series) is one of the most popular and affordable sliding window solutions available in India. This domal window features 27X65mm door dimensions with 1mm to 1.2mm wall thickness, making it perfect for residential and commercial applications. Engineered for smooth performance, practical ventilation, and long-lasting durability, this sliding window is ideal for balconies, bedrooms, living areas, and large openings.

2 Track or 3 Track Sliding Window Options

This sliding window offers flexible track options - choose 2 track (without mesh) for clean, modern full-view openings, or 3 track (with mesh) for comfortable ventilation without insects, dust, or pollution. The 3 track sliding window comes with two sliding glass panels + 1 sliding mesh, while the 2 track sliding window provides maximum glass area. Perfect choice for different room requirements - 3 track for balconies & bedrooms, 2 track for large living windows.

27MM Domal Series Specifications

This domal window uses 27X65mm door dimensions with 1mm to 1.2mm wall thickness, ensuring optimal balance between strength and affordability. The 27MM sliding window series is manufactured using 6063 T6 grade aluminium, offering high strength, rust-proof life, sound insulation, and smooth glide movement. Available in multiple color options including offwhite, white, black, grey, and brown, it perfectly suits both modern and traditional homes.

Height Recommendations

For best stability and performance, the maximum recommended height is 6 feet. However, you can go up to 8 feet at your own risk - doors won't break but may not remain completely stable and could shake/vibrate during sliding. This makes the 27MM domal window perfect for standard residential window openings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a domal window? What are the specifications of 27MM Domal Series?
Domal window is a popular and affordable sliding window series in India. The 27MM Domal Series features door dimensions of 27X65mm with wall thickness ranging from 1mm to 1.2mm. This sliding window series is perfect for residential applications, offering excellent balance between cost and performance. The 27MM domal window is available in 2 track (without mesh) and 3 track (with mesh) options.
What is the difference between 2 track and 3 track sliding window?
2 track sliding window has two tracks - one for each sliding glass panel, providing maximum glass area and clean modern look. 3 track sliding window has three tracks - two for glass panels and one for sliding mesh, offering ventilation without insects. 2 track is ideal for living rooms, 3 track is perfect for balconies and bedrooms where you want fresh air without bugs.
What is the maximum height recommended for 27MM domal sliding window?
For best stability and smooth sliding, the maximum recommended height is 6 feet. You can go up to 8 feet at your own risk - doors won't break but may not remain completely stable and could shake or vibrate during sliding. We recommend staying within 6 feet for optimal performance of your sliding window.
Which glass is best for 3 track sliding window?
For 27MM domal sliding windows, base includes 5mm clear tuff glass. You can upgrade to 6mm clear glass for better strength or 8mm clear glass for enhanced durability. For standard residential use, 5mm or 6mm glass works perfectly. Choose 8mm if you need extra strength for larger windows.
