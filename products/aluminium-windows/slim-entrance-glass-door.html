Frequently Asked Questions

Is a slim aluminium door safe?
Yes, with laminated/toughened glass + multipoint lock, it is fully secure. The 6063 T6 architectural-grade profile provides structural strength, while the multipoint locking system with premium SS components ensures maximum security. The high-impact glass options add an extra layer of protection.
Does it suit kitchens or outdoor spaces?
Perfect for kitchens, garden access, utility, balconies, lobbies. The weather-sealed gaskets provide excellent protection against dust, sound, and rain, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. The anti-rust powder coating ensures durability in various weather conditions.
Is it heat or sound resistant?
With DGU/laminated glass + gaskets, it reduces heat + blocks noise. The double-glazed unit (DGU) and laminated glass options provide excellent thermal insulation and sound reduction. Combined with weather-sealed gaskets, this door significantly improves indoor comfort by reducing heat transfer and external noise.
Does the frame bend or rust?
No. Aluminium never rusts and 6063-T6 is structural grade. The 6063 T6 architectural-grade aluminium is specifically designed for structural applications, providing exceptional strength and durability. Aluminium is naturally rust-proof, and the anti-rust powder coating adds an extra layer of protection, ensuring the door maintains its premium appearance for years without bending or corrosion.
