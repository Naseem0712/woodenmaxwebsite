Product Description

The Luxury Slim Entrance Glass Door features a premium 40mm super luxury slim series profile that creates a stunning 5-star modern resort look for your home entrance. This ultra-thin frame provides a full glass view while maintaining 100% security, weather protection, and long-term durability. Perfect for modern kitchens, utility areas, penthouses, lobbies, balconies, premium homes, and designer villas in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur.

40mm Super Luxury Slim Series Profile

This door features a 40mm super luxury slim series profile, which is the premium specification offering superior strength and aesthetics. The ultra-thin frame provides maximum glass visibility while maintaining structural integrity. This super luxury series is designed for high-end applications where both appearance and performance are critical.

8mm Toughened Glass & Slim Interlock Included

The base rate includes 8mm clear toughened glass and slim interlock, providing excellent clarity, safety, and seamless operation. You can upgrade to 10mm, 12mm, DGU (Double Glazed Unit), or Safety glass for enhanced performance. When you select 12mm, DGU, or Safety glass, the hardware automatically upgrades to premium multipoint hardware to support the heavier glass.

Maximum Height 10 Feet

This door is designed with a maximum height of 10 feet, ensuring optimal structural stability and performance. The height limitation ensures that the door maintains its integrity and smooth operation while providing ample clearance for most residential and commercial applications.

Premium Materials & Security

Made from 6063 T6 high-grade aluminium, this door offers exceptional strength and durability. Combined with premium toughened glass, slim interlock system, standard hardware, and optional multipoint or mortice lock systems, it provides maximum security for your home entrance. Optional security mesh is also available for added protection.
