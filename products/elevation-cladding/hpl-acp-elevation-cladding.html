Which is better for exterior ‚Äì HPL or ACP?
Both together look best: HPL for natural look + ACP for stylish highlight & strength. HPL provides the warmth and natural aesthetic of wood, while ACP offers crisp, contemporary finishes and structural strength. The combination creates a balanced, premium elevation that combines the best of both materials.
Does HPL fade in sunlight?
No. Exterior-grade HPL has UV protection and long life. The panels are specifically engineered with UV-resistant coatings that prevent fading, ensuring the natural wood appearance remains vibrant for 15-20+ years even under intense sunlight.
Is ACP safe for long-term outdoor use?
Yes. It's moisture-proof, rust-free & highly stable in heat and rain. ACP panels are designed for exterior applications and can withstand harsh weather conditions including heavy rain, intense heat, humidity, and temperature variations without warping, rusting, or degradation.
