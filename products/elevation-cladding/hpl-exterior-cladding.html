Frequently Asked Questions

Does HPL look like real wood?
Yes, it has realistic grain & texture without polishing or maintenance. The High-Pressure Laminate process creates authentic-looking wood patterns that are virtually indistinguishable from real wood from a distance, while providing superior durability and weather resistance.
Is HPL safe for exterior weather?
100% weatherproof, UV-proof & moisture-resistant. HPL panels are specifically engineered for exterior use and can withstand harsh Indian weather conditions including intense sunlight, heavy rain, humidity, and temperature variations without fading, swelling, or degradation.
Can HPL be used for gates & boundary walls?
Absolutely. It is strong, scratch-resistant & looks premium from both sides. HPL panels are ideal for gates and boundary walls as they provide a consistent, high-quality appearance on both sides, are resistant to scratches and impacts, and maintain their appearance for years without maintenance.
