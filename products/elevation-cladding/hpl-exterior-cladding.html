HPL Exterior Cladding ‚Äî Material Specs, Price & Installation Method

HPL (High-Pressure Laminate) is a revolutionary exterior cladding material that combines the timeless beauty of natural wood with the durability of modern technology. Manufactured by compressing multiple layers of kraft paper with phenolic resin under extreme heat and pressure (1400 PSI at 140¬∞C), HPL sheets achieve exceptional strength and weather resistance.

Unlike traditional wood that rots, warps, and needs constant painting, HPL panels maintain their stunning appearance for 25-30 years with zero maintenance. All brands provide 10-year warranty against color fading. The UV-stable surface resists fading even under harsh Indian sun, while the compact core prevents water absorption, swelling, and termite damage.

Professional Installation Framework

Our installation uses 50√ó25mm aluminium rectangle pipe framework with powder coating to match your selected HPL color. The frame is secured using rivets and 75√ó10mm stainless steel screws with wall plugs (gitti). For ceiling applications, we space the frame at 16-18" intervals for maximum support against gravity. For facade/wall applications, spacing is 22-24" as vertical surfaces require less dense support. Panels are installed horizontally (left to right) with vertical framing for facades, and cross-directional framing for ceilings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which HPL grade should I choose for my project?
Premium Imported (European) is ideal for luxury villas, main facades visible from road, and projects where budget isn't the primary concern. Standard Indian offers the best balance of quality and value for most residential and commercial projects. Economy Range works well for boundary walls, secondary elevations, and budget-focused projects. We're happy to recommend the best option for your specific needs ‚Äì just submit a quote request!
Why is 5% material wastage included?
HPL sheets come in fixed standard sizes. When cutting panels for your specific dimensions, some material is inevitably wasted at edges and corners. We also account for potential cutting errors and alignment adjustments. 5% is the industry-standard allowance to ensure you have sufficient material for a perfect finish without running short.
What's the difference between ceiling and facade installation?
Ceiling installation requires denser aluminium framework (16-18" spacing) because panels hang horizontally and must resist gravity. The frame runs in a cross-pattern for maximum support. Facade/wall installation uses standard spacing (22-24") since vertical panels have natural structural support. Frame runs vertically with panels installed left-to-right. Ceiling work requires more material and labor, hence the cost difference.
How are sheet requirements calculated?
We calculate your area, add 5% wastage, then divide by the standard sheet area (42.6 sq.ft for Premium/Standard, 32 sq.ft for Economy). The result is always rounded UP to the nearest whole sheet ‚Äì you can't buy partial sheets. Example: 90 sq.ft area + 5% = 94.5 sq.ft √∑ 42.6 sq.ft = 2.22 sheets ‚Üí 3 sheets required.
What warranty comes with HPL cladding?
All HPL exterior sheets come with 10-year warranty against color fading ‚Äì this warranty is provided directly by the brand manufacturers. For ceiling applications where sheets are protected from direct sunlight and rain, the expected lifespan is 25-30 years. Installation workmanship is covered under our standard 1-year warranty. Complete warranty documentation will be provided with your final quote.
