Frequently Asked Questions

Is 10mm toughened glass safe for shower?
Absolutely yes. 10mm toughened glass is 5x stronger than normal glass of the same thickness. It's safety-tempered, meaning if it ever breaks (which is extremely rare), it shatters into small, harmless pebble-like pieces rather than dangerous shards. It can withstand daily impacts, temperature changes, and heavy use.
What's the difference between Hinged and Sliding doors?
Hinged doors open outward like a regular door using hinges, handles, and L-connectors. Available in Mill Finish, Black, Gold, and Rose Gold. Sliding doors run on an aluminium top track with round knob handles and bottom guides. Sliding comes in Mill Finish, Matt Black, and Gold only. For L-corner showers, you can choose dual sliding doors.
What hardware finishes are available?
For Hinged doors: Mill Finish (Chrome), Black, Gold, and Rose Gold. For Sliding doors: Mill Finish, Matt Black, and Gold. Each hardware set includes all components needed for complete installation â€“ hinges/track, handles, seals, guides, and silicone.
What's the difference between L-corner and straight?
L-corner configuration is for corner showers where glass panels meet at 90Â°. You'll need to measure both the left and right side widths. Straight configuration is for showers along a single wall â€“ just one width measurement needed. Both use the same premium glass and hardware, and our calculator adjusts automatically based on your selection.
What seal types are available?
We provide T-seal, F-seal, Magnetic seal, and D-type seal options â€“ all included in the hardware set at no extra cost. Seals are available in Black or Clear colors. Magnetic seals provide the best water-tight closure, while T and F seals work great for fixed panels. Our team will recommend the best option based on your configuration.
Does frameless shower glass leak water?
When installed by professionals with proper seals, frameless shower doors have minimal to no water leakage. Our magnetic seals create a tight closure, and the silicone application at wall junctions prevents seepage. The key is expert installation and proper seal selection â€“ both of which we provide with every project.
What is the maximum height for frameless shower?
Standard height is 7 feet, and maximum height is 8 feet for frameless installations. For heights beyond 8 feet, the glass becomes too heavy for standard hardware and requires custom solutions. Contact us if you need heights above 8 feet â€“ we can provide custom engineering for special requirements.
