Why towel handle instead of knob?
Towel handles offer dual functionality â€“ you can hang a towel on them while also using them to open/close the door. They provide a better grip, especially with wet hands, and look more elegant than basic round knobs. It's a practical upgrade that enhances both usability and aesthetics.
What hardware finishes are available?
Available in Matt Black, Gold, and Mill Finish. Matt Black is the most popular for modern bathrooms. Gold adds luxury appeal. Mill Finish provides a classic chrome-like appearance. All finishes are applied using high-quality coating processes for durability.
