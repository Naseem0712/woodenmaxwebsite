Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is soft-close sliding?
Soft-close is an advanced damping mechanism built into the sliding track. When you push the door, it slides normally. But as it approaches the open or closed position, the dampers automatically slow down the door and bring it to a gentle, silent stop. No slamming, no noise â€“ just smooth, luxury operation every time.
Can I get dual sliding doors?
Dual sliding doors are available for L-Corner configurations only. This allows both sides of the L-corner to have their own sliding door for maximum convenience. Straight showers use a single sliding door as they only have one entry point.
What's the difference between profile sizes?
Regular Slim profiles are 16x45mm â€“ a perfect balance of strength and aesthetics. Ultra Slim profiles are 16x35mm â€“ the most minimalist look with thinner visible frames. Both are imported aluminium with the same quality finish and corrosion resistance. Choose based on your aesthetic preference.
What hardware finishes are available?
Available in Mill Finish (silver), Matt Black, Gold, and Rose Gold. Mill Finish is the most economical. Matt Black and Gold are standard premium options. Rose Gold is the most premium with additional cost for both glass and hardware â€“ perfect for luxury interiors.
Is openable door option available?
This product is specifically designed for soft-close sliding operation only. For openable (hinged) doors with the same profiles and finishes, please check our Premium Black Profile Shower which offers openable doors with towel handle and 3pc hinges.
