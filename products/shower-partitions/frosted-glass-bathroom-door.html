Is frosted glass safe for bathroom doors?
Yes. This door uses 8mm toughened safety glass, which is 5x stronger than normal glass and safe for wet areas. The toughened glass is specifically designed for bathroom applications and provides superior safety compared to standard glass.

No. Frosted glass provides 100% privacy. Only shadows are visible, not details. The frosted finish ensures complete privacy while still allowing natural light to pass through, creating a bright and comfortable bathroom environment.
