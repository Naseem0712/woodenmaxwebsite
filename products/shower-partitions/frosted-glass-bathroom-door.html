Frequently Asked Questions

What is fold & slide door and how is it different from sliding?
A fold & slide door has 2 panels that fold against each other and slide to one side. This gives you 95% clear opening ‚Äì almost full access. Regular sliding doors only give 50% opening because one panel always blocks half the space. Fold & slide is the best choice when you need maximum opening with space efficiency.
What's the difference between clear and frosted glass?
Clear glass is fully transparent ‚Äì you can see through it completely. Frosted glass provides 100% privacy ‚Äì only shadows are visible, not details. For bathrooms and toilets, frosted glass is recommended. For staircases and general entrances, clear glass works well. Frosted glass has a small additional cost.
Is lock included or extra?
Lock is an optional add-on. The basic package includes all hardware for smooth operation (track, rollers, gasket, handle, seals) but not the lock. If you need a lock (recommended for bathrooms and toilets), you can add it as an extra. This gives you flexibility to choose based on where you're installing the door.
Can I use this door for staircase?
Absolutely! This door is perfect for staircases. It provides a safety partition while allowing light to pass through. The 95% opening is great for carrying items up and down. Many customers use it for staircase entrances, duplex floor separations, and terrace access points. For staircases, clear glass is usually preferred.
What hardware colors are available?
Hardware is available in White, Matt Black, and Gold. All three colors have the same price ‚Äì no extra charge for any color. Choose the color that matches your bathroom or interior d√©cor. Matt Black is most popular for modern interiors, Gold for luxury homes, and White for minimal designs.
