Frequently Asked Questions

What is the profile size for aluminium louver canopy?
We use 50x50x1.5MM 6063-T6 grade aluminium profiles. These heavy-duty square profiles are installed every 8 inches (200mm) gap to provide optimal shade coverage while allowing air circulation.
Can the canopy withstand heavy wind and rain?
Yes, absolutely. Each canopy is designed with structural engineering calculations for safety and durability. The heavy-duty 50x50x1.5MM aluminium profiles and robust mounting system are engineered to withstand wind loads, rain, and harsh weather conditions.
Why is aluminium better than wood or steel canopy?
Aluminium offers unmatched advantages: no rust like steel, no termites or rot like wood, no repainting required, and no structural bending over time. The zero-maintenance benefit makes aluminium the most cost-effective long-term solution.
How much heat reduction does louver canopy provide?
The louver canopy reduces direct solar heat gain by 30-40% while maintaining natural ventilation. The angled blades block harsh sunlight and create shade while allowing air to flow through.
Can the canopy design be customized?
Fully customizable. We can adjust louver angle, blade spacing, projection length, overall shape, and mounting style to match your architectural vision. Choose from cantilever, floating frame, or angular mount installations.
