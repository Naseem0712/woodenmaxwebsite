Frequently Asked Questions

Can the canopy withstand heavy wind and rain?
Yes. Designed with structural engineering load calculations for safety & durability. The high-strength aluminium/steel hybrid structure is engineered to withstand wind loads, rain, and other weather elements. Professional installation ensures proper anchoring and structural integrity for long-term performance.
Can we customize the shape and angle?
Fully customizable as per architectural drawings & sun direction analysis. We can adjust the louver angle, spacing, projection length, and overall shape to match your design requirements and optimize sun shading based on the building's orientation and location.
Is it better than wood or steel canopy?
Aluminium = no rust, no termites, no bending, and no repainting. Unlike wood which requires regular maintenance and is prone to termites and rot, or steel which rusts over time, aluminium provides superior durability with zero maintenance. The powder-coated or PVDF finishes last 20-30 years without fading or degradation.
Does it reduce heat inside the building?
Yes. Louvers reduce solar heat gain while allowing natural ventilation. By blocking direct sunlight and creating shade, the canopy significantly reduces heat entering the building through windows and facades. The angled louvers also allow air to flow through, maintaining ventilation while providing protection from sun and rain.
