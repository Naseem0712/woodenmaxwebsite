Product Description

The Wooden Finish Aluminium Louvers transform your home or commercial building into a luxury architectural masterpiece. This elevation design features premium aerofoil and flat-profile aluminium louvers, finished in a rich wooden texture coating, giving the beauty of wood with the strength of metal.

Versatile Application

These louvers work both horizontally & vertically, offering shade, privacy, ventilation, and stunning visual detailing. Ideal for balconies, front facades, roofs, office fronts, and contemporary villa designs, this system provides a modern international look without any maintenance like wood.

Natural Look, Zero Maintenance

Perfect for premium villa exteriors, resorts, cafes, and office buildings. The wooden texture coating provides a natural wood appearance while maintaining all the benefits of aluminium - rust-free, termite-proof, fire-resistant, and maintenance-free for 20+ years.

Modern Architectural Style

This design gives your building a Dubai/Greece architectural style look, adding significant value to modern homes and commercial projects. The louvers can be used as roof pergola, partition, and elevation fins, providing both aesthetic appeal and functional benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do wooden finish louvers fade outside?
No. They come with UV-protected coating, suitable for outdoor use. The high-quality wooden texture finish is specifically designed to resist UV rays and maintain its appearance for decades, even in direct sunlight. Unlike real wood, these louvers never fade, peel, or require repainting.
Are aluminium louvers stronger than wood?
Yes. Aluminium is rust-free, termite-proof, fire-resistant & long-lasting. Made from 6063 T6 structural-grade aluminium, these louvers offer exceptional strength and durability. They don't rot, warp, or get damaged by insects like wood does. The material is also fire-resistant, making it safer for building applications.
Can we customize the shade design?
Yes. Multiple profiles, spacing, thickness & colors are available. You can choose from aerofoil, flat, or box louver profiles, customize the width (50mm to 200mm), adjust spacing between blades, and select from various finish options including wooden texture, anodized, or powder-coated colors. We can create custom designs to match your architectural vision.
Can it work as a privacy screen?
Yes. It provides air + shade + privacy, perfect for balconies. By adjusting the blade spacing and angle, you can achieve varying levels of privacy while maintaining airflow. This makes it ideal for balconies, terraces, and outdoor spaces where you want privacy without compromising ventilation or natural light.
What is the price range for wooden finish aluminium louvers in Hyderabad, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Jaipur?
Price varies based on louver type (aerofoil/flat/box), profile size, finish option (wooden texture/anodized/powder coated), mounting complexity, and project size.
