Product Description

The UPVC Sliding Window Series 3000 is our best-selling window, combining premium materials with superior sound insulation. Perfect for homes near busy roads, airports, or commercial areas.

Saint-Gobain Glass

We exclusively use Saint-Gobain glass in all configurations. Single glass (5-12mm) for standard applications, DGU glass (16-22mm) for enhanced thermal and sound insulation, or laminated glass for superior safety and soundproofing.

Imported Hardware

All hardware components are 100% imported, ensuring smooth operation, superior security with multi-point locking, and long-lasting performance. The imported hardware requires minimal maintenance.
