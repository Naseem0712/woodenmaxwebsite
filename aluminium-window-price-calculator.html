How to Use Aluminium Window Price Calculator

Our free aluminium window price calculator helps you estimate costs for sliding windows, casement windows, and other aluminium window types instantly. Follow these simple steps to get accurate price estimates:

Step 1: Enter Window Dimensions

Input your window size in feet, meters, or any preferred unit. The calculator automatically converts to square feet for accurate pricing. You can enter dimensions in multiple formats:

  • Feet: Enter width and height in feet (e.g., 6 feet width, 4 feet height)
  • Feet + Inches: Use format like 6'8 for 6 feet 8 inches
  • Meters: Enter in meters for metric measurements
  • Centimeters/Millimeters: For precise measurements

Step 2: Select Number of Windows

Specify how many windows you need. The calculator multiplies the per-window cost by the number of windows to give you total project cost. This is especially useful for:

  • Multiple windows in same room
  • Complete home window replacement
  • Commercial building projects

Step 3: Choose Glass Type

Select the glass type based on your requirements:

  • 6mm Clear Glass: Base option, included in standard price
  • 8mm Clear Glass: Additional cost applies - Better strength and durability
  • 10mm Clear Glass: Additional cost applies - Premium option for large windows
  • 12mm Clear Glass: Additional cost applies - Maximum strength for extra-large openings
  • DGU 20mm: Additional cost applies - Double glazed unit for energy efficiency and sound insulation
  • Safety Glass 13.52mm: Additional cost applies - Laminated safety glass for high-rise buildings

Step 4: Select Coating Type

Choose your preferred finish:

  • Texture/Smooth Finish: Standard option, included in base price
  • Wooden Finish: Additional cost applies - Natural wood appearance without maintenance

Step 5: Choose Lock Type

Select security level:

  • Single Point Lock: Standard touch-type lock, included
  • Multi-Point Lock: Additional cost applies - Enhanced security with handle-type operation

Different profile series have different price points:

Frequently Asked Questions

How is aluminium window price calculated?

Aluminium window price calculation starts with calculating the window area (width × height). Then the base rate per square foot is multiplied. Glass type, profile series, coating, and hardware costs are added. The calculator automatically calculates actual wastage based on standard glass sheet sizes (typically 8x10 feet or 10x12 feet) and aluminium profile cutting optimization. This ensures you only pay for materials actually used, not a fixed percentage. Final price = (Area × Base Rate) + Glass Cost + Coating Cost + Hardware Cost + Material Wastage (calculated from actual cutting).

Is sliding window price less or more than casement window?

Generally, sliding window price is slightly less than casement window because sliding windows use a simple track system, while casement windows require complex hardware (hinges, multipoint locks). However, final price depends on glass type, profile series, and hardware selection. Adding DGU glass or premium hardware to either option increases the price.

When should DGU glass be used?

DGU (Double Glazed Unit) glass should be used when: (1) Balcony or windows face direct sunlight - DGU reduces heat transfer by 40-50%, (2) High-rise buildings - for sound insulation (30-40 dB noise reduction), (3) To reduce AC bills - DGU improves energy efficiency by 20-30%, (4) Extreme weather areas - DGU prevents condensation. DGU glass adds additional cost, but provides long-term electricity bill savings. DGU is highly recommended in cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore.

How much does price vary between Hyderabad and Delhi?

Price between Hyderabad and Delhi generally varies by 5-10% due to: (1) Transportation cost - Material transport cost is slightly higher in Delhi, (2) Labor rates - Labor rates are slightly higher in Delhi, (3) Market competition - Competition is higher in Hyderabad, making prices more competitive. Base price remains consistent, but installation and transportation charges vary. The calculator shows base price - final quote includes city-specific adjustments. Exact city-wise pricing is available after free site visit.

Is warranty and installation cost included?

The price shown in the calculator includes installation cost (5-10% of total cost). Warranty is completely free - 10 years warranty on profiles, glass, and hardware. Installation includes: site measurement, frame fixing, glass installation, hardware fitting, sealing, and testing. Extra charges apply only when: (1) Site preparation is required (additional cost), (2) Custom size (non-standard dimensions - additional percentage applies), (3) High-rise installation (special equipment - additional cost). Standard installation is completely included in the calculator price.
